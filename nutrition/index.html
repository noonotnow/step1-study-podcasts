🎯 High-Yield Concepts

  • Glycolysis: Net 2 ATP (anaerobic); glucose → 2 pyruvate → acetyl-CoA (aerobic).
  • β-Oxidation: Occurs in mitochondrial matrix; requires carnitine shuttle for long-chain FAs.
  • Kwashiorkor vs. Marasmus: Protein deficiency vs. total calorie deficiency — edema is the key differentiator.
  • Niacin (B3): Deficiency → Pellagra (3 D's: Dermatitis, Diarrhea, Dementia).
  • Thiamine (B1): Cofactor for pyruvate dehydrogenase, α-ketoglutarate DH, transketolase.
