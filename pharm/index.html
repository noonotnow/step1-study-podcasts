🎯 High-Yield Concepts

  • Muscarinic receptors (M1-M5): M2 (heart — ↓HR), M3 (smooth muscle, glands — SLUDD). Gi-coupled (M2), Gq-coupled (M1, M3).
  • Atropine: Muscarinic antagonist — "Hot as a hare, dry as a bone, red as a beet, blind as a bat, mad as a hatter."
  • β1 vs β2: β1 = heart (1 heart). β2 = lungs, uterus, vasculature (2 lungs). β1 blockers = metoprolol, atenolol.
  • α1 agonists (phenylephrine): Vasoconstriction → ↑BP → reflex bradycardia (no β effect).
  • Organophosphate toxicity: Irreversible AChE inhibition → SLUDGE + DUMBELS. Treat with atropine + pralidoxime (2-PAM).
  • Epinephrine: α1, α2, β1, β2. Used in anaphylaxis, cardiac arrest, open-angle glaucoma.
