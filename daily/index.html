🎯 Today's High-Yield Facts

  • Thiamine (B1): Deficiency → Wernicke's encephalopathy (confusion, ophthalmoplegia, ataxia) → Korsakoff's (confabulation). Give thiamine before glucose in alcoholics.
  • SnNout: Sensitive test, when Negative, rules Out disease. SpPin: Specific test, when Positive, rules In disease.
  • Encapsulated organisms: S. pneumoniae, H. influenzae, N. meningitidis, E. coli, Group B Strep, Klebsiella, Salmonella. Asplenic patients: vaccinate against these.
  • HFrEF (systolic HF): EF <40%. Dilated heart. Drugs with mortality benefit: ACEi/ARB, β-blocker, spironolactone, SGLT-2 inhibitors (newer).
  • HFpEF (diastolic HF): EF >50%. Stiff/hypertrophied ventricle. Control hypertension and rate. No mortality-reducing drugs firmly established.
