🎯 High-Yield Concepts

  • Insulin: Secreted by β-cells (islets of Langerhans). Promotes glucose uptake (GLUT4), glycogen synthesis, lipogenesis, protein synthesis. Inhibits gluconeogenesis and lipolysis.
  • Type 1 DM: Autoimmune destruction of β-cells. HLA-DR3/DR4. Absolute insulin deficiency. Prone to DKA.
  • Type 2 DM: Insulin resistance + relative insulin deficiency. Associated with obesity. First-line = metformin (↓hepatic glucose production).
  • DKA: Type 1 DM. Anion-gap metabolic acidosis, hyperglycemia, ketonemia. Treat with IV fluids, insulin, K⁺ repletion.
  • HHS: Type 2 DM. Extreme hyperglycemia (>600), hyperosmolarity, NO ketosis. Higher mortality than DKA.
  • Hypothyroidism: ↑TSH, ↓T4. Hashimoto's = most common cause in developed world (anti-TPO antibodies).
